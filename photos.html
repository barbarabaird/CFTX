Photos


MisFit Mayhem 2015

MisFit Mayhem is a women only competition that is put on by CrossFit Round Rock



Battle of the Bell

Battle of the Bell is a friendly competion between CrossFit TX, VooDoo CrossFit, 512 and CrossFit City Limits. It is held 3 times a year with each box hosting it one of those times





Murph (Veteran's Day WOD) 2015

Murph is a WOD done on Memorial Day weekend each year. It is named after LT. Michael P. Murphy. It is done in rememberance of those who have served our Country



Barbells for Boobs (B4B) Charity WOD 2014

Barbells for Boobs is a fundraising campaign to raise money to pay for mammograms. It is held each year during October.





Halloween Fun 2014

Here at CrossFit TX we like to have a little fun as we are putting in hard work. One of the fun things that we do each year is a Halloween WOD, where we all dress up and have a great time together.
