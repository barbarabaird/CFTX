Directions

From Gattis School Rd:

Take Gattis School Rd headed East. Turn Right onto Priem Ln and take the curve to the left and then turn into the industrial park on the left. Instead of turning onto Priem Lane you could continue on and take a right onto the HWY 130 access road, you will then take a right onto Priem Lane and then turn right into the industrial park.

From HWY 130 North bound:

Take the Gattis School Rd exit. When approaching Gattis School Rd take the turn around and head South on the access road. Turn right onto Priem Ln and then right into the industrial park.

From HWY 130 South bound:

Take the Gattis School Rd exit. Turn right onto Priem Ln and then right into the industrial park.
